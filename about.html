Chickpotle began with a simple dream: what if you could have your waffle fries and eat your guac too? Well, now you can. The application is a demonstration of HTML5/Javascript principles and uses various Google Maps and Places APIs to find the optimized pair of Chick-fil-A and Chipotle restaurants based on your location. The product was developed in the summer of 2018 by students from the University of Georgia Master's of Business Technology program.

Note: We are not affiliated with Chick-fil-A or Chipotle. Please dont sue us!
