Chickpotle began with a simple dream: what if you could have your waffle fries and eat your guac too? Well, now you can. Chickpotle uses HTML5 to choose the optimal Chick-fil-A and Chipotle pair based on your current location. No more driving across town looking for the giant pepper logo after you bagged your 12-count nuggets!
